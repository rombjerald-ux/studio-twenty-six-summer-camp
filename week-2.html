Week 2 • Georgia O'Keeffe

Nature Lab / Nature & Texture

Theme: Observation, Biology & Natural Design. Students study nature like scientists and artists at the same time.

Day By Day

Nature week moves from observation into texture, scale, engineering, and earth materials.

Monday • Observational Drawing

Flower studies

STEAM: scientific observation skills, visual analysis, and attention to detail used in biology and medical illustration.

Tuesday • Texture + Surface

Clay leaf impressions

STEAM: environmental science, texture mapping, and natural structures found in ecosystems.

Wednesday • Scale

Large-scale flower pastels

STEAM: proportion, magnification, and scale concepts used in microscopy and scientific modeling.

Thursday • Nature Engineering

Found-object mobiles

STEAM: balance, weight distribution, suspension, and kinetic engineering concepts.

Friday • Earth Materials

Driftwood sculpture

STEAM: sustainability, material reuse, environmental design, and sculptural construction.

Extras

This week adds nature walk sketchbooks, flower arranging, and sand/clay sensory table time.

Best For

Kids who love flowers, nature walks, texture, and tactile making.

Drop-In Fit

Good drop-in week, especially midweek texture and scale days.

Friday Finish

Families see sculptural work and more grounded, material-rich projects.
