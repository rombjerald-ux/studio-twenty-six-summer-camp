How This Camp Works

This is a real studio camp, not random crafts to fill time. Kids move through artist lessons, project blocks, reflection, and end-of-week sharing.

Studio Day

Structured, but not stiff.

Sketchbook warm-ups, slideshows, major projects, second creative blocks, outdoor breaks, and closing gallery share.

Flexible Entry

Full weeks, multiple weeks, or drop-ins.

Because the season is late, families can request single-day placements when there is room and the week still makes sense for the child.

Payment Options

Pay ahead for the discount, or pay as you go.

Families can reserve a fuller block up front or use flexible payment for partial weeks and drop-ins. The registration form captures which route you want.

What Drop-Ins Mean Here

Drop-ins are not a second-tier option. If there is room, campers can join for a single day or a few days late in the season. We will place them in a week where the projects and flow still make sense.

Use the registration form to mark full week, partial week, or single-day interest.
