FAQ

Answers for families who need to decide quickly.

This page is built for late-season registration too, so the flexible enrollment questions are answered directly.

Can this all be handled from one QR code?

Yes. The intended setup is one QR code pointing either to the camp homepage or directly to the registration page. From there families can understand the offer, choose a week, and submit one simple form.

What ages is camp for?

The program is designed for ages 5-17. When you register, include your child’s age so we can group and guide projects appropriately.

Can we still join if it is late?

Yes. Families can request full weeks, partial weeks, or drop-in days. We confirm placements based on capacity and whether the current week works well for a late entry.

What counts as a drop-in?

A drop-in can be a single day or a few selected days in a week. It is especially useful for late-season signups, travel schedules, or families testing the program before committing to more.

Are supplies included?

Yes. Core supplies are included, along with sketchbooks, collaborative materials, and project support.

What is the schedule?

Camp runs Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 2 PM. The day includes sketchbook warm-up, artist lesson, project blocks, outdoor break, reflection, and closing share.

Do kids need art experience?

No. The camp is built for a wide range of experience levels. Curiosity is enough.

Can we pay ahead and get a discount?

Yes. Families who know their weeks in advance can indicate that they want the pay-in-full option, and the follow-up can reflect the advance-pay discount.

Can we pay as we go?

Yes. That works especially well for drop-ins, partial weeks, or late-season decisions. Mark pay-as-you-go on the form and note whether you are requesting weekly or per-day flexibility.

How do sliding scale requests work?

Mark it on the registration form or write it in the notes. We follow up directly rather than making families jump through a separate process.

How do we ask about a specific week?

Use the registration page and select the weeks you want. If you are looking for a drop-in during one specific week, mention the exact days in the notes field.

Still deciding between full week and drop-in?

If the season timing is tight, register with the most realistic option and use the notes field to explain what flexibility you need. We can sort the fit from there.
