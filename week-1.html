Week 1 • Henri Matisse

Color Lab / Color & Joy

Theme: Bold Color, Shape & Freedom. Students explore how artists and designers use color psychology, spatial awareness, pattern, and composition to communicate ideas and emotions.

Day By Day

Every day has one clear focus, one major project, and one STEAM connection.

Monday • Color Theory

Giant painted color wheels

STEAM: light absorption, visual perception, and emotional psychology of color used in branding, design, and marketing.

Tuesday • Shape + Geometry

Cut-paper collages

STEAM: geometry, positive and negative space, symmetry, spatial planning, and engineering through composition building.

Wednesday • Pattern Design

Textile-inspired paintings

STEAM: mathematical repetition, sequencing, and pattern systems used in fashion, coding, and architecture.

Thursday • Composition

Collaborative mural

STEAM: scale mapping, teamwork, visual problem solving.

Friday • Wearable Art

Painted hats or tote bags

STEAM: product design, functionality, material science, and creative entrepreneurship.

Extras

This week also includes fruit still-life drawing, a painted jazz session, and an outdoor watercolor picnic.

Best For

Kids who love bold color, collage, fashion, and visible results fast.

Drop-In Fit

Strong drop-in week because each day stands on its own well.

Friday Finish

Families get a mini show with wearable pieces and mural energy.
