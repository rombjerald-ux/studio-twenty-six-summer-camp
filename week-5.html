Week 5 • Pablo Picasso

The Big Art Studio / Creative Studio Lab

Theme: Experimentation, Innovation & Independent Thinking. Students work like real artists, designers, inventors, and collaborators.

Day By Day

This is the most open-ended week, built for choice, confidence, and a stronger exhibition close.

Photo slot: abstract faces, sculpture towers, giant canvas, gallery show.
Monday • Portrait Construction

Abstract faces

STEAM: facial geometry, proportion, and analytical thinking.

Tuesday • Structural Sculpture

Cardboard towers

STEAM: engineering, balance, support systems, and construction methods.

Wednesday • Collaborative Painting

Giant canvas

STEAM: teamwork, large-scale planning, and project coordination.

Thursday • Open Studio Design

Independent project

STEAM: self-direction, innovation, problem solving, and creative confidence.

Friday • Exhibition Design

Gallery show

STEAM: presentation design, curation, public speaking, and entrepreneurial skills.

Extras

This week also includes artist interviews, a mini art market, DIY frames, and a collaborative installation piece.

Best For

Kids who like more autonomy and want to end camp with a bigger public finish.

Drop-In Fit

Best for campers who can do at least several days, especially for exhibition build-up.

Friday Finish

The biggest parent-facing close of the season, with curation and gallery energy.
