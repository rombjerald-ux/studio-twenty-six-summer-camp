Week 3 • Jean-Michel Basquiat

Street Art / Self Expression

Theme: Identity, Communication & Urban Design. Students explore visual language, symbolism, and public art while learning how design shapes cities and culture.

Day By Day

This week is built for kids who want louder marks, stronger symbols, and more public energy.

Monday • Mark Making

Graffiti lettering

STEAM: typography, branding, communication systems, and graphic design principles.

Tuesday • Symbolism

Crown self portraits

STEAM: visual storytelling and icon systems used in logos, advertising, and digital media.

Wednesday • Layering

Mixed-media cityscapes

STEAM: urban planning, architectural layering, and systems thinking.

Thursday • Text + Art

Poetry posters

STEAM: literacy, communication, design hierarchy, and emotional expression.

Friday • Collaboration

Giant mural wall installation

STEAM: teamwork, engineering of large-scale projects, planning, and leadership skills.

Extras

This week also includes a hip hop drawing session, DIY sticker making, and painted skate decks or cardboard guitars.

Best For

Kids who want expressive projects, words, symbols, and bold visual identity.

Drop-In Fit

Very strong for drop-ins because each day lands as a standalone project.

Friday Finish

The week ends with a giant collaborative wall and visible group energy.
