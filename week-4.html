Week 4 • Salvador Dali

Invention Lab / Dreams & Surrealism

Theme: Innovation, Problem Solving & Creative Thinking. Students learn that imagination is essential to invention.

Day By Day

This week gives imaginative kids more room to invent and tell stories through objects and scenes.

Photo slot: dream journals, clay creatures, dioramas, puppet theatre.
Monday • Idea Development

Dream journals

STEAM: brainstorming, conceptual thinking, and innovation practices used in entrepreneurship and product design.

Tuesday • 3D Form

Clay creatures

STEAM: structural engineering, volume, stability, and sculptural mechanics.

Wednesday • Space Design

Diorama dream rooms

STEAM: architecture, interior design, scale models, and spatial planning.

Thursday • Transformation

Surreal object redesign

STEAM: adaptive thinking, innovation, and industrial design concepts.

Friday • Storytelling Systems

Puppet theatre

STEAM: mechanics, storytelling, performance engineering, and collaborative production.

Extras

This week also includes costume day, shadow projections, and a collaborative dream cave.

Best For

Kids who want dream logic, creatures, narrative, and weird ideas made physical.

Drop-In Fit

Good drop-in week, especially Tuesday through Thursday.

Friday Finish

The week closes with story, performance, and puppet-theater energy.
